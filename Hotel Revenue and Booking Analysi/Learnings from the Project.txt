What i have learned from this project 

From this project, I learned:

Guest Satisfaction Insights:

Identified key factors affecting guest satisfaction through detailed feedback and average rating analysis.
Realized the importance of maintaining high service standards across different cities and room categories.

Operational Performance Monitoring:

Gained insights into occupancy rates and their impact on resource allocation and service delivery.
Understood the significance of tracking maintenance and housekeeping performance for overall guest satisfaction.
Revenue Optimization:

Learned how to use revenue metrics like ADR and RevPAR to develop effective pricing strategies.
Recognized the importance of analyzing revenue by booking platforms and city to enhance targeted marketing efforts.
Booking Trends Analysis:

Discovered patterns in booking behavior by day type and platform, which can be leveraged to design better promotional offers.
Analyzed booking data by room category to understand guest preferences and improve room allocation strategies.
