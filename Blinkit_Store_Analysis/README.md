<h2>Dashboard Features</h2>
    <ul class="features">
        <li><strong>Sales by Fat Content</strong>: This section allows users to compare the performance of items based on their fat content (low fat vs. regular fat), helping in understanding customer preferences for healthier options.</li>
        <li><strong>Sales by Item Type</strong>: Identifies the top-performing item types, enabling Blinkit to focus on high-demand products.</li>
        <li><strong>Sales by Outlet Type</strong>: Analyzes sales performance across different outlet types (e.g., Supermarkets, Grocery Stores), providing insights into which types of outlets drive the most revenue.</li>
        <li><strong>Sales by Outlet Location Type</strong>: Evaluates sales distribution across different geographical locations (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3 cities), helping to identify regional trends and target areas for expansion.</li>
        <li><strong>Sales Trends Over Time</strong>: Tracks the growth of sales over a specific period, allowing stakeholders to identify seasonal trends and make informed inventory decisions.</li>
        <li><strong>Outlet Performance Metrics</strong>: Provides detailed metrics for each outlet type, such as total sales, average sales per transaction, and customer satisfaction ratings, helping to identify top-performing outlets and areas for improvement.</li>
    </ul>

<h2>How to Use</h2>
    <p>
        <strong>Filter Panel</strong>: Use the filters on the left side of the dashboard to drill down into specific data segments, such as outlet location types, outlet sizes, and item types.<br>
        <strong>Interactive Visualizations</strong>: Click on different sections of the charts to explore detailed insights. The dashboard's interactive nature allows users to uncover hidden patterns and correlations within the data.
    </p>
