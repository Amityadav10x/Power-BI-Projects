Objectives

Understand Sales Distribution: Identify which item types and outlet types contribute most to total sales.

Analyze Customer Preferences: Evaluate customer preferences based on item fat content and ratings.

Monitor Outlet Performance:
 Track the performance of different outlet types and locations over time.

Identify Growth Trends:

 Analyze sales trends over the years to identify periods of high growth.
Improve Inventory Management: 

Use insights from item type performance to optimize inventory levels and reduce wastage.
